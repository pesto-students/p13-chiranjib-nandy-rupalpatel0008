Gandhinagar, India

Location

Gandhinagar is located near Ahemedabad in the state of Gujarat in India

About

Weather

Its warm in the seasons of summer and monsoon but very cold in the season of winter.

Population

It has a population of 8,772,000 people.

Sports

Cricket is the most popular sports in Gandhinagar.

Things To Do

  • Visit the Indroda Park
  • Visit the Mahatma Madir
  • Enjoy the traditional Gujarati snacks at the restaurant Pooja located in sector 21.
  • Roam in the gardens near GH4
